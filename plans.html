Unit Plan

This unit plan was created for a 7th grade, 10-week, “Choices and Consequences” unit based around the novel Tangerine. The unit plan maps out a daily learning objective, AZ 7th grade ELA standards tie-in, and task analysis for each day of the unit. There are two major embedded assessments: a literary analysis essay, and biographical research project, involved in this unit; both provide valuable context for reading and analyzing Tangerine. This unit, as facilitated by the SUSD English department, is meant to foster respect for diversity among students, a lasting love for literature, and provide a rigorous, project-based curriculum that will see all students succeed. All assignments, a copy of the unit plan, and other materials are available through Google Classroom, enabling active parent/student engagement throughout the semester. (InTASC Standards 4, 5, 7)

Lesson Plan

This lesson plan was created as part of a 10-week “Choices and Consequences” unit based around the novel Tangerine. This lesson plan contains a “Learning Target” and “Success Criteria” in tandem with SUSD’s planning norms and expectations. As well as meeting specific learner literacy requirements, the lesson works to continue the promotion of equity and student engagement, and provides a well-managed learning environment for all students at all times.(InTASC Standards 3 and 7)
