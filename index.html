My Teaching
Philosophy

I believe every student has the right to the highest quality education possible. Through education every goal is achievable, and every student has the right to reach their utmost potential. I work tirelessly to inspire the educational aspirations in all my students.

I believe a teacher is more than a classroom instructor. A teacher is a role model, a life coach, and always has an open door and time for every student. A teacher is a living example of empathy and respect.

I believe a teacher prepares their students for continued education and life outside of school. I believe my students will always receive tools for their future endeavors.

I believe a teacher can affect and embody positive change each day, and I know that as a teacher I am a molder and influencer of positive growth for all my students.
