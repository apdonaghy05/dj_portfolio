About

Whether it is inside or outside of a classroom, nothing beats witnessing a student’s aha moment. That is why in addition to teaching Language Arts I try and serve as a coach for sports teams and clubs whenever possible. In the past five years I have coached High School and Elementary-aged basketball teams, a Middle School baseball team (that’s me – back row, second from right), and served as founder and lead teacher of a “Rock ‘n’ Soul Music” performance elective class at ACE Charter High School.

I spent my teenage years playing soccer and baseball on travel teams in the Pacific NW, culminating with a chance to represent my country playing in tournaments overseas. In my twenties I played in multiple touring rock ‘n’ roll bands, performing in cities and towns across the country. I love helping students positively interact with the world around them by intertwining my passion for sports and music with formal pedagogy whenever possible.
